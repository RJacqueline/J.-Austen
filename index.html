A little bit about me.

I'm a native Iowan who has made her home in Chicago, London, Los Angeles and New York City. My journey into tech started with a marketing education. Seeking to burn some creative energy, I decided to create my own digital magazine, which then inspired me to to learn how to build my own site to fit my own specific needs.

After learning how to build a website, I now have the freedom & versatility I was looking for in my work. Now I've made a full switch to design and front-end development, where I can use my organization skills and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code and design elegant websites.
